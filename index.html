About The Game

A maze puzzle built on direction and logic.

Arrows Escape takes the concept of mazes and transforms it into a directional puzzle challenge. Instead of walking through paths, you control the direction itself. Every arrow influences the next movement, and each rotation changes the board. You'll need patience and a good eye for detail to master these puzzles.
It’s relaxing at first — until the maze gets clever.

How to Play

Simple mechanics. Smart decisions.

  • Step 1

    Tap or rotate arrows

    Adjust each arrow to create a connected path. The orientation determines where movement flows next.

  • Step 2

    Clear obstacles

    Some levels include barriers or blocked routes. You must rotate arrows strategically to open the correct escape path.

  • Step 3

    Escape the maze

    Complete the correct sequence to guide the path out of the maze. A single mistake may force you to rethink the entire layout.
