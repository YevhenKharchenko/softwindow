How to Play

Simple mechanics. Smart decisions.

  • Step 1

    Tap or rotate arrows

    Adjust each arrow to create a connected path. The orientation determines where movement flows next.

  • Step 2

    Clear obstacles

    Some levels include barriers or blocked routes. You must rotate arrows strategically to open the correct escape path.

  • Step 3

    Escape the maze

    Complete the correct sequence to guide the path out of the maze. A single mistake may force you to rethink the entire layout.
